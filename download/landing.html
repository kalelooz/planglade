The origin story

I needed one place where I could see everything — tasks, notes, timelines, people — without switching tabs or losing the thread. That place didn’t exist the way I wanted it.

So I built it. FlowBoard started as a weekend project and grew into something I use every single day. Visual, connected, and fast to navigate.

It’s open source. Self-host it for free and own your data completely, or wait for the cloud version if you’d rather not manage a server. Either way — it’s yours.
