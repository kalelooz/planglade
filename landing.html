Product Architecture

Local-first today, server-backed tomorrow.

FlowBoard operates completely within your local browser sandbox, utilizing indexable storage databases and in-memory structures to guarantee instantaneous reactions. Since there is no database handshake or network lag, it remains fully functional offline, keeping your raw documents, tags, and milestones private to your device.

Engineering Roadmap Statement: Cross-device sync is actively in progress.

Our active engineering roadmap is presently establishing core workspace APIs and server synchronization channels. Until stable database-backed authentication layers are rolled out, your active workload state is kept entirely on your local machine. We believe in perfecting robust, low-latency client mechanics before introducing cloud sync complexity.
