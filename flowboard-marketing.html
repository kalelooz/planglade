Status: Client-Side Prototype

Local-first today. Sync coming tomorrow.

Currently, FlowBoard operates entirely within your browser's local sandbox. This guarantees zero latency and complete privacy for your immediate tasks.

While the application proves the core workflow models, please be aware that cross-device synchronization and team collaboration features are not yet available in the current prototype. We are actively building the stable server persistence layer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about the product's current capabilities.

Can I collaborate with my team?

Not yet. FlowBoard is currently optimized as a solo workspace. Team sharing and role-based permissions are planned for future database-backed iterations.

Is there a mobile app?

The interface is responsive and works on mobile browsers, but dedicated native apps with offline persistence are not part of the initial prototype.

Will my data disappear?

The prototype relies on local browser storage. Clearing your browser data will wipe your workspace. We highly recommend using FlowBoard for active testing rather than mission-critical long-term storage until cloud sync is released.
