# Code of Conduct

PlanGlade should be a practical, respectful place to build.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep feedback specific and useful.
- Welcome people with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Focus disagreements on ideas, tradeoffs, and code.
- Respect privacy and do not share private information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Sharing secrets, tokens, private data, or exploit details in public project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct concern and no dedicated contact is published yet, use a private maintainer contact channel when possible. If no private channel exists, open a minimal public issue asking for a private contact path without sharing sensitive details.
